Tesseract

A fast, private home screen for Android.

A Pixel-style launcher with app folders, Android 15 private space, notification dots, and anonymous Kagi search — with no trackers, no ads, and nothing sent to a server.

Built for speed and privacy

Everything stays on your device.

Pixel-style home & drawer

A clean, familiar layout with an adaptive grid, swipe-up app drawer, and wallpaper-aware labels.

App folders

Drag one app onto another to make a folder, then reorder and organize apps inside it however you like.

Private space

Reveal or hide your Android 15 private space straight from the drawer — unlocked by the system, not us.

Anonymous Kagi search

Built-in Kagi search with Privacy Pass, so your searches can be unlinked from your Kagi account.

Notification dots

An optional dot on apps with pending notifications. Read on-device only and never stored or sent.

Widgets & shortcuts

Place and resize home-screen widgets, and long-press any icon for its app shortcuts.
